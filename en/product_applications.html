Other applications

The materials of Flexform Korea can be used in various areas of our daily lives.

  • Transportation

    The interior/exterior materials for aerospace, ships, train, and others.

  • Geotextile

    Geotextile materials for reinforcement, drainage, filtering, or erosion prevention.

  • Agricultural

    Nonwoven for heat-preservation/insulation covers or weed-control.

  • Household products

    Shelf trays, TV panels, absorbent, road surface protection nonwoven materials, and many more daily-life applications.
