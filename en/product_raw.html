Raw material

  • Natural fiber

    We use natural fiber materials available in nature and mineral fibers to make eco-friendly materials.

    Kenaf Cotton Hemp Pond basalt copper
  • Synthetic fiber

    Synthetic fibers are chemical synthesized fiber materials, including the commercial plastics and the engineering plastics that are developed as a substitute for metal.

    PP PET PE Nylon PU
  • Functional fiber

    Functional fibers have additional functions based on synthetic fibers. The fibers with functions such as sound absorption, flame retardancy, and water repellency etc. can be applied according to their purpose and use.

    Hollow fiber Water-repellant fiber Flame-retardant fiber Carbon fiber Bico Aramid

FAQ

  • We can use up to four different types of fibers per 1 layer. and we can produce multi-layered composite materials, so more fibers can be used depending on the structure of product.

  • We can produce up to 200 to 3,000g/m2. The thickness of a board after the DBP process can be up to 10T (depending on the density of the nonwoven).

  • The cotton shoddy made by recycling old clothes or the recycled plastic materials such as recycled PP or PET.

  • We use raw materials that serve as binders when heated and it melt and compress at high temperature and high pressure in our DBP facility, so there is no need to use adhesives.

  • It’s a Double steel Belt Press, which is used to heat up and press down nonwoven fabric materials to make boards.

  • Any single fabric material can be used, including knit or woven materials other than non-woven materials.

  • The board has the advantages of sound absorption, impact resistance, insulation, and a light weight due to its micro-porous structure.

  • We use dyed raw materials and adjust a mixing ratio of raw materials to provide the colors of the customer's requirements.

  • We produce nonwoven with the needle-punching

  • It is a type of bast fiber, which is a natural material that is mainly produced in the subtropical climate of Bangladeshi. Since it is a natural material, it is possible to make eco-friendly products with it.
